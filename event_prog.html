Event Programme 2016

The Festival has walks and activities programmed each day including the highly rated forest walks during the weekend. The programme caters for a range of interests and abilities from beginners to experts. The extensive programme includes many walks and activities hosted by a number of Rotorua community organisations, volunteers and tourism operators.

Note that the 21 and 42km walks on Saturday and the 20 and 30km walks on Sunday are IML Association accredited walks. All walks are IVV accredited (except the short nocturnal walk).

IML and IVV WalksProgramme Schedule - 19 to 20 March 2016

FRIDAY 18th MARCH 2016

4.00pm - 8.00pm
Check-in or register and collect registration packs at the Registration Centre, Neil Hunt Park, Tarawera Road, Rotorua.

5.15pm - 6.15pm
Mayoral Reception and Official Opening for all walkers - Rotorua Lakes Council Offices (upstairs), Fenton Street. (Lake End)

6.15pm - 7.15pm
Short walk and street parade leaving from Rotorua Lakes Council. All local and overseas walkers and friends invited.

Saturday 19th MARCH 2016

Day ONE Walks

START TIME Saturday - 42Km @ 8.00am, 21Km @ 9.00am, 10Km @ 10.00am

42Km & 21Km marathon walks and 10Km walk. These walks will follow routes through the "Redwood" and "Whaka" Forests and include some of New Zealand's finest bush and forest scenery.

12 noon - 3pm
Live entertainment on stage at Walk HQ at Neil Hunt Park

SUNDAY 20 MARCH 2016

Day TWO Walks

START TIME Sunday - 30Km @ 8.00am, 20Km @ 9.00am, 10Km @ 10.00am.

30Km, 20Km, 10Km walks . These walks will follow routes largely through the city, parks, thermal reserves, lakeshore, and points of historical interest.

12 noon - 2.30pm
Live entertainment on stage at Walk HQ

2.30pm - Closing Ceremony
A short closing function and farewell to all NZ and Overseas participants. Presentation of significant IML and NZ awards, team trophies and spot Prize presentations.

START TIMES

Saturday - 42Km @ 8.00am, 21Km @ 9.00am, 10Km @ 10.00am

Sunday - 30Km @ 8.00am, 20Km @ 9.00am, 10Km @ 10.00am.
